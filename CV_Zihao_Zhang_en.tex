\Section{Profile}
{\color{Muted}\fontsize{8.5}{11.5}\selectfont
I received my M.S. degree in the School of Computer Science at Fudan University, advised by \href{https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=f3_FP8AAAAAJ\&hl=zh-CN\&authuser=1\&oi=ao}{\textcolor{myblue}{\underline{Prof. Yu-Gang Jiang}}} and \href{https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=7t12hVkAAAAJ\&hl=zh-CN\&authuser=1}{\textcolor{myblue}{\underline{Prof. Zuxuan Wu}}}.

\vspace{0.2em}
My research explores generative visual modeling for \textbf{embodied intelligence}, with the goal of building \textbf{controllable and efficient visual foundations} that help embodied agents understand and predict how scenes evolve over time.

\vspace{0.2em}
My work has been published at leading computer vision and multimedia venues, including \textbf{CVPR}, \textbf{ICCV}, \textbf{ACM MM} and \textbf{SIGGRAPH Asia}.\par
}
